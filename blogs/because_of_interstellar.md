#### Why ASCII?

The donut ascii has always been on the back of my mind, and I recently came upon it while revisiting old memories. I was intruiged by how characters can be placed with simple math to create intricate renderings. As such, my love for interstellar and considental reminder of the donut.c led me to a unified idea, a black hole but in ASCII!

Also, it's cool becuase after doing a little research, the first image(or visual) was done by a [CNRS researcher as early as 1979]("https://www.researchgate.net/publication/234464726_Image_of_a_spherical_black_hole_with_thin_accretion_disk"), and really you will see below how similar my results are with this. Each dots kind of look like ASCII, but now rotating!
