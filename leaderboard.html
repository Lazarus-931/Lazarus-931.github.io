How scoring works

Each kernel is benchmarked against its MLX baseline on the same chip, same input shapes, across 200 iterations after warmup. Three numbers go in:

  • Speedup = MLX median time / Metal kernel median time. Greater than 1.0 means the kernel beats MLX.
  • Stability = 1 − (max − min) / median across iterations. 1.0 means perfectly consistent runs.

The composite score shown on the right side of each row combines them as:

score = 0.8 × speedup + 0.2 × stability
